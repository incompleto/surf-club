Club

The participants will surf the web for 15 minutes each in front of a small audience using the services, themes or devices they like the most.

It will be on June 20th 19:30 in Madrid. La nave nodriza, Doctor Fourquet, 27.

Surf club is a free entry event, limited seats available.
